Electricity Consumption Analysis in India

Electricity consumption is a critical indicator of economic growth, industrial development, and living standards. This project analyzes electricity consumption patterns across 33 states and 5 regions of India during the period 2019–2020.

The dataset contains 16,599 records with state-wise monthly electricity usage data. The analysis focuses on consumption trends before, during, and after the COVID-19 lockdown period, highlighting the economic and social impact on energy demand across India.
